Kaoru Seta

Seta Kaoru is a first-year student at Yotsuba Women's University, as well as the guitarist of Hello, Happy World! She is very popular with ladies, whom she dubs her "little kittens" (子猫ーちゃん, koneko-chan). Himari and Rimi in particular have a tendency of blushing in her presence. Outside of the band, she is also Chisato's childhood friend, and their parents are good friends as well.

Hagumi Kitazawa

Kitazawa Hagumi is an energetic third-year student at Hanasakigawa Girls' High School and the bassist of Hello, Happy World! Outside of the band, she is also friends with Saaya.
